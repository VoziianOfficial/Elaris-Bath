Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Elaris Bath uses cookies and similar technologies when homeowners visit this website.

Last updated: April 24, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember preferences, support functionality, and understand how visitors interact with pages.

2. How We Use Cookies

Elaris Bath may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Support basic website functionality
  • Remember cookie consent preferences
  • Understand website performance and user activity
  • Improve forms, navigation, and user experience
  • Support advertising, analytics, and lead-routing operations

3. Types of Cookies We May Use

  • Essential cookies: needed for basic site functionality.
  • Analytics cookies: help us understand website usage and improve performance.
  • Advertising cookies: may help measure campaigns or support relevant advertising.
  • Preference cookies: may remember choices such as cookie consent.

4. Third-Party Technologies

We may use third-party analytics, advertising, call tracking, form, or lead-routing tools. These third parties may use cookies, pixels, scripts, or similar technologies according to their own policies.

5. Your Cookie Choices

You may accept or decline non-essential cookies through the cookie banner where available. You can also control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Browser Controls

Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or limit cookies. If you disable cookies, some website features may not work as expected.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Cookie Policy, contact us at support@elarisbath.com.
