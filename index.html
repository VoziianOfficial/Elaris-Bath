Before requesting quotes

Questions that make provider comparison easier.

What exact bathroom work is included? Are materials, fixtures, and disposal listed? Who confirms local permit requirements? What license and insurance details apply? How long should the project take? What could change the quoted price? Is cleanup included in writing? What warranty or follow-up terms apply? Does the provider serve your area?
