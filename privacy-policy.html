Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Elaris Bath collects, uses, and shares information when homeowners use this website.

Last updated: April 24, 2026

1. Who We Are

Elaris Bath is an independent bathroom provider matching platform. We are not a bathroom contractor, installer, plumbing company, renovation company, or direct service provider.

2. Information We May Collect

When you submit a form, request quotes, call, email, or interact with this website, we may collect information such as name, email address, phone number, project type, project notes, approximate service area, browser data, and website usage data.

3. How We Use Information

We may use submitted information to process homeowner requests, connect homeowners with independent local service providers, respond to inquiries, improve website performance, support advertising and analytics, and comply with legal obligations.

4. Sharing Information With Providers

By submitting a request, you understand that your information may be shared with independent local bathroom service providers or related partners who may contact you about your project.

5. Independent Providers

Contractors and providers are independent third parties. We do not control their business practices, pricing, availability, qualifications, licenses, insurance, workmanship, or project outcomes.

6. Cookies and Tracking

This website may use cookies, analytics tools, advertising pixels, and similar technologies. You can review more details in our Cookie Policy.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@elarisbath.com.
