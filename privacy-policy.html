Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Elaris Bath collects, uses, and shares information when homeowners use this website.

Last updated: April 24, 2026

1. Who We Are

Elaris Bath is an independent bathroom provider matching platform. We are not a bathroom contractor, installer, plumbing company, renovation company, or direct service provider.

2. Information We May Collect

When you submit a form, request quotes, call, email, or interact with this website, we may collect information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Project type
  • Project notes or message content
  • Approximate location or service area information
  • Browser, device, and website usage data

3. How We Use Information

We may use submitted information to:

  • Process homeowner requests
  • Connect homeowners with independent local service providers
  • Respond to inquiries
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Support advertising, analytics, and lead-routing operations
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

4. Sharing Information With Providers

By submitting a request, you understand that your information may be shared with independent local bathroom service providers or related partners who may contact you about your project.

5. Independent Providers

Contractors and providers are independent third parties. We do not control their business practices, pricing, availability, qualifications, licenses, insurance, workmanship, or project outcomes.

6. Cookies and Tracking

This website may use cookies, analytics tools, advertising pixels, and similar technologies to improve the website and understand user activity. You can review more details in our Cookie Policy.

7. Your Choices

You may choose not to submit personal information through the website. You may also adjust browser settings to limit cookies or tracking technologies.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information, but no website, transmission, or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Children’s Privacy

This website is intended for adults and homeowners. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Lead Routing and Matching Operations

When you request quotes or submit a bathroom project inquiry, we may use the information provided to categorize the project, identify potentially relevant service providers, and support follow-up communications. This may include sharing request details with independent partners that may serve your area.

11. Advertising and Measurement

We may use advertising or measurement tools to understand which pages, forms, or campaigns help users find the platform. These tools may collect device, browser, page-view, and interaction information.

12. No Contractor Relationship

Submitting information through this website does not create a contractor relationship with Elaris Bath. Any service agreement is made directly between the homeowner and the independent provider selected by the homeowner.

13. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@elarisbath.com.
